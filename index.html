01 — About IDR

Built by Industry.
Designed for Risk.

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute purpose-built for professionals operating at the frontier of digital risk, cyber security, and AI governance. We work exclusively in regulated and high-consequence environments — where the cost of getting risk wrong is measured in real outcomes.

IDR bridges the gap between academic research and industry practice by combining leading UK university expertise with the judgment of active senior practitioners. Our graduates don't just understand risk theory — they arrive deployment-ready, having contributed to live industry challenges from their first weeks of training.

04 — Community

A Network Built
for the Risk Frontier.

IDR is more than a training provider — it is a professional community for those building careers in the most demanding risk environments. Whether you are a student entering the field for the first time or a seasoned practitioner shaping policy, IDR connects you with a network of peers, mentors, and employers who hold the same high standards.

Our community spans all career stages: from undergraduates exploring digital risk, through early-career analysts building their first frameworks, to established professionals taking on advisory roles.
